Project One

High-resolution Weak Signal Detection

Design a system by mixing the unknown signal with a continuous linear changing local oscillator signal, a difference frequency signal will be sent to the stochastic resonance system. Thus, an obviously changed output can be obtained. Due to stochastic resonance system is extremely sensitive to low frequency, the system will have a maximum output when the local oscillator and frequency of the unknown signal are closest. The frequency of the unknown signal will be measured precisely.

Project three

Unhealthy Vegetation Detection

This project explores a segmentation method, modify excess green index with Otsu' method, to divide the different pixel of the scene into vegetation and background that consist of sand, silt, dry clay and atmosphere shadow. And create a user friendly application for video processing. The comparative study was completed to test the realizable of vegetation segment in remote sensing system.
